About ANIX

Where luxury meets artistry, and tradition embraces innovation

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Milan in 1985, ANIX began as a small atelier with a singular vision: to create handbags that transcend mere accessories and become timeless works of art. Our founder, Isabella Marchetti, was inspired by the rich tradition of Italian leather craftsmanship and the desire to create pieces that would be treasured for generations.

What started as a passion project in a small workshop has evolved into one of the world's most prestigious luxury handbag brands. Yet, despite our growth, we have never forgotten our roots. Every ANIX handbag is still crafted with the same attention to detail, the same commitment to excellence, and the same love for the craft that Isabella instilled from the very beginning.

Today, ANIX represents the pinnacle of luxury handbag design, combining traditional Italian craftsmanship with contemporary innovation. Our pieces are coveted by discerning women worldwide who appreciate the finest things in life.

The Art of Craftsmanship

01

Material Selection

We source only the finest leathers from renowned tanneries in Italy and France. Each hide is carefully inspected and selected for its quality, texture, and natural beauty.

02

Pattern Creation

Our master pattern makers create precise templates that ensure perfect proportions and optimal use of materials. Each pattern is a work of art in itself.

03

Hand Cutting

Skilled artisans hand-cut each piece with precision, ensuring that the grain and texture of the leather are perfectly aligned for maximum beauty and durability.

04

Expert Stitching

Using traditional saddle-stitching techniques, our craftsmen create seams that are not only beautiful but will last a lifetime. Each stitch is placed with meticulous care.

05

Hardware Assembly

Premium hardware, often plated in gold or crafted from sterling silver, is carefully attached using time-honored techniques that ensure both beauty and functionality.

06

Final Inspection

Every handbag undergoes rigorous quality control. Only pieces that meet our exacting standards bear the ANIX name and are worthy of our customers.

Our Values

Sustainability

We are committed to responsible sourcing and sustainable practices. Our leathers come from ethically managed farms, and we continuously work to minimize our environmental impact.

Artisan Heritage

We preserve traditional craftsmanship techniques while providing our artisans with fair wages and excellent working conditions. Their skill and dedication are the heart of our brand.

Uncompromising Quality

We never compromise on quality. From the selection of materials to the final stitch, every aspect of our handbags reflects our commitment to excellence.

Timeless Design

Our designs transcend fleeting trends. We create pieces that will be as beautiful and relevant decades from now as they are today.
